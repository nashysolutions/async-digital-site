Audient

On-device audio capture, transcription, and semantic search for macOS. Your recordings stay on your Mac.

Mac App Store (coming soon) Privacy policy

What it does

  • Records audio on your Mac and stores it locally
  • Transcribes recordings using OpenAI Whisper, running on-device via WhisperKit
  • Builds a semantic index with a bundled sentence-embedding model so you can search across what was said, not just the file names
  • Keeps everything under ~/Library/Application Support/Audient/ — no cloud sync, no telemetry SDKs, no third-party services

Status

  • In development — not yet on the Mac App Store
  • The privacy policy is published now so it is in place at the moment of App Store review
