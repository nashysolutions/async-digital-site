What clinics get

A reply within seconds

Every missed caller hears back within seconds, even when the line is busy or out of hours.

Self-serve callbacks

Callers pick a callback time that suits them, so the diary fills around existing appointments rather than across them.

Bookings in a calendar, not a new tool

Each callback lands in a small calendar we set up. The receptionist treats it like any other appointment, and calls back on the existing line.
