The problem

Missed calls, lost patients

  • Receptionists are busy with patients in the chair
  • Callers hang up and try the next clinic on Google
  • You never know how many enquiries you missed

Manual follow-up doesn't scale

  • Checking voicemail between appointments is hit-or-miss
  • Callbacks get forgotten on busy days
  • No system means no consistency

Every missed call has a cost

  • A new patient enquiry can be worth hundreds of pounds
  • First impressions matter. Silence says "we're too busy for you."
  • Competitors who respond faster win the booking

How Speed to Lead works

  1. We detect the missed call

    When a call goes unanswered, our automation picks it up immediately. No hardware to install. It works alongside your existing phone line.

  2. The caller gets a follow-up

    Within seconds, the caller receives a friendly message with a link to book a callback at a time that suits them.

  3. The booking lands in your diary

    The callback appears on your calendar automatically. Your team calls back, the patient books in, and nothing gets lost.
